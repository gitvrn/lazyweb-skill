TOP RECOMMENDATIONS (do first):
1. Make the "you lost your streak" screen supportive + recoverable: acknowledge the break, then offer a one-tap lower-effort path back in (Gentler "Go Gentler", Sweatcoin "lower the goal") instead of a pure loss message.
2. Ship a streak-protection economy: a Streak Freeze (pre-emptive) AND a Streak Repair (retroactive buy-back), surfaced in a rewards store with coin prices and a PRO/free-monthly perk (Mimo); show freezes earned/used on the streak dashboard (Duolingo, Drops "rescues").
3. Celebrate the streak with a full-screen flame + weekly day-tracker confirmation and a single forward CTA; reserve the loud celebration for day-1 and named milestones (Storia, Finch, Falou, Couple Joy, TikTok).

INDEX ON: (a) recoverable loss framing with a lower-effort re-entry action; (b) a two-part freeze/repair economy tied to coins/PRO; (c) flame + 7-day-row + single-CTA celebration layout.
DO NOT OVER-INDEX ON: Snapchat's Snapstreak-restore (buried in a paid feature list, social-messaging context, off-category) and TikTok messaging streaks (social DM, not habit/self-care) — treat both as adjacent inspiration, not the model. 1 · Treat streak-loss as recoverable, not a verdict

Highest leverage for retention and lowest risk. The one app in the corpus that explicitly tells you the streak is gone (Gentler) immediately offers a lower-effort way back in ("Go Gentler"), and Sweatcoin's "missed multiple days" alert leads with "lower the goal." The anti-pattern is a celebratory-styled loss screen or a dead end. Directional · loss notice in 1 of 61 screens · no outcome data

B. Streak-protection economy — freeze (pre-emptive) + repair (buy-back)

Strong seen in 7 of 61 screens Directional · 7/61 · no outcome data

The dominant recovery mechanic: let users protect a streak before it breaks (Freeze) and restore one after (Repair / Rescue). Best version is a priced store with a free-monthly PRO perk; lighter versions surface earned freezes and usage right on the streak dashboard.
