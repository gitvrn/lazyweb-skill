TOP RECOMMENDATIONS (do first):
1. Frame streak loss as a supportive reset, not a failure: empathetic copy + an immediate restart CTA in the same view (see Gentler "you lost your streak" screens).
2. Offer a concrete recovery mechanic next to the loss message — Streak Freeze / Streak Repair / Streak Save / Rescue — gated on coins or PRO (see Mimo rewards store, Drops Rescues, Alma Streak saves, Duolingo freezes).
3. Celebrate milestones with a full-screen confetti/mascot moment that fires at day 1 AND at round milestones (28/100), with a single forward CTA (see Duolingo, Flo, me+, Finch, Storia).

Streak loss framed as a supportive reset

Directional seen in 2 of 2 explicit-loss screens Single-source · Gentler only

The only real "you lost your streak" screens in the corpus both soften the blow: supportive guidance to update status or start a short session, a visible streak/progress bar, and a restart path in the same view — never a red error. Both come from one app (Gentler), so treat the exact pattern as single-source and validate the copy.

Recovery mechanic: Freeze / Repair / Save / Rescue

Directional seen in 5 of ~70 references Directional · 5/~70 refs · no outcome data

The recovery counterpart to a loss: a way to protect or repair the streak, usually gated on an in-app economy (coins) or a PRO tier. Naming varies — Freeze, Repair, Save, Rescue, Vacation mode — but the mechanic is the same. Attach it directly to the loss/at-risk moment rather than burying it in a store.

Full-screen milestone celebration (confetti / mascot)

Strong seen in 7 of ~70 references Directional · 7/~70 refs · no outcome data

The positive bookend to streak loss. A dedicated celebratory screen fires both at the very first day (to hook the habit) and at round milestones (28, 100 days). Pattern: big number + flame/confetti/mascot art + one short congratulatory line + a single forward CTA. This is the most consistently repeated pattern in the corpus.

Calendar + records view of the streak

Directional seen in 6 of ~70 references Directional · 6/~70 refs · no outcome data

The persistent "home" for the streak: a monthly calendar of practiced days plus current/best records. After a loss this view shows the gap honestly while keeping prior records visible — useful context, but lower priority than the loss and celebration moments and not worth showing for a brand-new streak.

Anti-pattern: gating progress behind a "hit a 3-day streak to unlock"

Avoid (for loss/recovery) seen in 1 of ~70 references Single-source · off-moment

Locking the dashboard behind a streak threshold is fine as a cold-start hook, but applied to a loss/recovery flow it punishes the user at the exact moment they're most likely to churn. Shown here so the skip decision is evidenced, not just asserted — use the supportive-reset pattern instead.
