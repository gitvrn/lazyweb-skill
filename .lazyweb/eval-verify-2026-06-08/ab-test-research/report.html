A/B Test Research: Lifting Annual-Plan Share on a Hard Paywall

Education / language learning (Duolingo-class) · hard paywall, annual vs monthly · goal: grow annual-plan mix without tanking overall subscribe / trial-start rate · 2026-06-08

FOR THE CODING AGENT

Restructure the hard paywall to pre-select a discounted annual trial against a higher-ARPU anchor with one clear Start-Free-Trial CTA — that is the single highest-leverage test for lifting annual share without scaring off subscribers.

LAZYWEB REPORT — AGENT HANDOFF
Use this report as a starting point for prioritizing and shipping annual-vs-monthly hard-paywall experiments grounded in what comparable apps have already tested.

TOP RECOMMENDATIONS (do first):
1. Restructure the plan selector to lead with a high-ARPU lifetime anchor while pre-selecting a discounted annual trial with a single Start-Free-Trial CTA (rests on exp drops fae32674).
2. Attach a short free trial (7-14 days) to the annual plan so the higher yearly price reads as low-risk, then watch trial-start and refund guardrails (rests on exp blinkist 5abb41ba, elevate 7ca0f42b, mimo 1764fcb1).
3. Make the trial / annual path unmistakable on the card itself — a Free-Trial badge or ribbon on the pre-selected annual card — before the user parses pricing (rests on exp gauthmath c1790900).

Strong / Weak points

Strong

  • Uses generic _experiments evidence across screen types rather than paywall-only retrieval.
  • Returns control and variant screenshot IDs so agents can inspect visual evidence before recommending changes.

Weak

  • Observed screenshot-diff evidence is not a statistically measured experiment readout.
  • Outcome lift is only available when captured in the experiment learning text.
  • interesting_learning defaults false because uncommon learnings can overfit this limited dataset.

Where not to overgeneralize

  • This corpus is mobile-subscription-centric and these are directional screenshot-diff signals, not measured lift — no row reports a win/loss number or significance.
  • Two of eight experiments (blinkist, elevate) sit in Study Aids / Flashcards, and gauthmath is Homework & Math Help; only drops is true language learning. Read cross-app patterns as hypotheses to localize on your own hard paywall, not as guarantees for a Duolingo-class audience.
  • Several moves cut price or commitment (gauthmath exp c07e3f9c swaps the annual plan for a cheaper 6-month plan and a lower monthly). Those can lift overall conversion while lowering annual share — the opposite of this goal — so treat them as guardrail context, not as things to copy.
  • Most rows have no platform tag; do not assume the layout transfers cleanly between iOS, Android, and web checkout without a per-surface test.
